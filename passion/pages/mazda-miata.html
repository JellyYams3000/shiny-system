BETH

Beth is a free spirit that shops at thrift stores, farmers market. She's very eco-concious so she only uses paper bags and doesn't allow plastic into her home. She's also kind of a health freak and would go out of her way to spend a fortune on different diets that she heard is healthier for the body, mind, and soul. She works at a nursing home and particularly loves to read to the elderly, saying it's calming for both her and the residents. She's often asked, "Is Beth short for Bethany?" and she always answers, "Just Beth" in an airy voice. unfortunately, she is a little gullible and often falls for pranks, jokes, and media only meant for trolling. She's a big beach person and often spends her vacations tanning under violent UV rays. She also takes pottery classes on the weekends because she loves the idea of making all of her cups, bowls, and plates.
