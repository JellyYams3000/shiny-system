TIFFANY (goes by Tiff)

Tiff is a wealthy teenager that was promised an expensive car when she graduates high school. She lives and breathes in pink and pastel colors and draws a lot of her style from the movie 'Mean Girls.' She has a white Shih Tzu that's scraggly and dirty but she loves him to death, and if she can, she will bring him along on shopping trips. Tiff is an og and downloaded musically before Tik Tok was even a thing. She was an active poster and was proud of her 53 followers. Despite giving off a 'popular girl' vibe, she is the nicest person you'll meet, and she will go out of her way to help anyone. She enjoys volunteering at homeless shelters because she loves the connection she makes with the people there and helping at the same time. She is an extrovert and doesn't understand the idea of introverts and often adopts them and talks enough for the both of them.
