ANNE (NOT ANNIE)

Anne is the oldest sibling and takes that role VERY seriously. She takes charge of trips, books all the flights, hotels, and reservations. She doesn't trust people to do things that she knows she can do better. She prefers to drive her friends around because she doesn't trust other people to drive. She's very serious about how people pronounce her name and she gets really angry if it's pronounced differently. She always has to have the newest gadget in her house and technology is a big part of her lifestyle. Whenever a new phone is release, she has to get it, and everything is automated in her house, from her lights, to her sound system, even to her appliances (her electricity bill is THROUGH THE ROOF). She absolutely hates Halloween because she doesn't like kids knocking on her door at night and she could care less about buying and giving out candy. She loves to spend HOURS in Michael's.
