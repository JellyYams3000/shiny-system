MICHAEL

Michael is a freshly graduated college student that already found a job in sales. He absolutely HATES his job but had no other choice but to go for it because income is better than no income (a very practical man). He's the type that doesn't like kids around and doesn't appreciate it when they invade his space. He's very particular about his personal bubble and only have a few people in his inner circle. His girlfriend on the other hand is the complete opposite and people often had doubts about how their relationship would work out but they beat the odds and are happily together. In his free time, he enjoys woodworking and works on random and creative projects (he likes to keep his hands busy). He often keeps a sketchbook nearby for when imagination strikes and will immediately start drawing whatever it is. He likes to make his own juice because he doesn't trust the ones from the store. He will religiously have a cup of fresh juice in the morning before he starts the day because it starts the day on a refreshing note. Do not give him coffee, he hates that stuff.
