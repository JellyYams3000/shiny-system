ROBERT (DO NOT call him Bobby)

Robert is a middle aged man that loves to go to bars, orders a beer, and watches whatever is on the TV for a few hours before he goes home. He always has a frown on his face and people often describe him as unapprochable. Despite that, he is actually a great conversationalist and will often have deep conversations with anyone no matter the topic. He has a very soft spot for rabbits and has 3 of them at home. He has stray cats roaming around his house, so he keeps some of the cat food tubes in case they come around and want a snack. He has a wife that gardens, so you will often find him in Home Depot buying soil, mulch, and whatever supplies she may need. He has his own little corner in her garden because he wanted to try growing herbs (so far he has some mint, rosemary, and basil going). After he gets off of work, you can find him nappong in his recliner chair with the TV on in the background.
