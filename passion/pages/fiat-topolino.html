THEODORE

Theodore is an awkward little fella that just entered 7th grade. He has a Five Nights at Freddy's hyperfixation and draws FNAF in all his math worskeets. He has a hard time focusing in school and he HATES math, he can never make sense of the numbers and formulas on his homework. Instead, he chooses to spend his time creatively, expressing himself through drawing and painting (he recently got into textiles and is enjoying learning crochet). He has a small knit friend group, choosing to spend their time watching movies, playing video games, and going roller skating in the park. He recently started washing cars in the neighborhood because he wants his own snack money for whenever he hangs out with his friends. He is allergic to strawberries and is TERRIFIED of bees.
