BUZZ

Buzz is a junior in college studying marketing. He's meticulous about how his environment is organized and doesn't like it when anything is out of place. He likes to keep up a cool boy persona but he is extrmely nerdy about history and likes to put history documentaries on in the background when he's doing chores. He's recently gotten into art history. To keep up his "cool boy" persona, he goes to parties that his friends invite him too but doesn't drink too much, instead he prefers a glass of some cold sweet tea. He is a huge procrastinator when it comes to school work, if he is given 2 weeks to finish an assignment, he won't do it until the last minute. Despite that, he has excellent grades with a 3.6 GPA. On the weekends when he's free, He goes to the gym, cooks a nice meal (he has a book with all his recipes), puts on Indiana Jones, with a dessert to end the night.
