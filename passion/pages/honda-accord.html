SHERYLL

Sheryll has to have her morning coffee to start her day, you can not talk to her if she hasn't had her coffeee yet. When she goes to a coffee shop, she always orders an EXTRA HOT latte with 4 shots of espresso (that she pronounces EXpresso). She has 6 gandkids that he spoils to high heaven and her kids are tired of dealing with that. She has an aggressive pet peeve when grocery store baggers put soft food under heavy food and will throw a fit at the register. She religiously gets her car washed and detailed because her car is her baby but doesn't want the work cleaning to fall completely on her. She has a million rugs all over her house, plates hung up on the wall, and souveirs from every place that she's been to. She loves to collect salt and pepper shakers and has a gajillion of them stuffed in their own special cabinet. She always ends the day with a bottle of diet coke.
