JOHNNY

Johnny is a stable 47 year old man. He works at a tech company making a decent amount to support his wife and two kids. It's very likely that on the weekends he would take the family to go fishing or hiking because he believes that movement is good for the body, also because he hates that he sits in a cubicle 5 days a week. He hates parties, he can't do crowds and loud music; the most he could do is a backyard party with friends and family. He LOVES his mustache, takes care of it day and night everyday of the week because he believes a strong mustache enhances his masculinity and that's something he takes pride in. In his free time, he is always renovating something or finding something to fix around the house and (unfortunately) ropes his kids into helping. At the end of the day, he sits on his porch drinking a glass of whiskey neat. It has to be neat because a hard liquor is a represention of a strong man and cocktails are too feminine.
